Hello, I'm Stuti

I am Stuti, a Masters student of Environmental Studies at the University of Pennsylvania, passionate about creating sustainable solutions for our planet's most pressing environmental challenges. My journey in sustainability began with a deep curiosity about the intersection of environmental science, policy, and community engagement.

With a background in Mass Communication and hands-on experience in ESG research, rural development, and climate change studies, I bring a unique perspective to environmental problem-solving. My work spans from analyzing corporate sustainability practices to implementing grassroots environmental initiatives in rural communities.

Currently pursuing my Master of Environmental Studies at Penn, I'm focused on developing innovative approaches to environmental challenges while contributing to meaningful research through the Environmental Innovations Initiative. I believe in the power of combining academic knowledge with real-world application to drive positive change.

When I'm not immersed in research or sustainability projects, you can find me exploring sustainable food options around University City, planning my next eco-friendly travel adventure, or sharing insights about environmental issues through my writing and social media.
